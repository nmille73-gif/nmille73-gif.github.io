About Wes Packham

Wes Packham is a Junior Computer Science student at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. After joining the Niner Times back in 2024, Wes became interested in pursuing photography in his spare time. From there, his interest bloomed into passion, and he soon started covering events and taking photos for Niner Times usage.

Ever since, his passion has only grown. To him, it's very much a hobby that he wishes to continue to improve, and the best way to do that is by practicing as much as possible. By serving his local community and gaining hands-on experience taking differently styled photos for a variety of different occasions, he hopes to expand his skills along with his portfolio.
