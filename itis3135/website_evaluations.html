Website Evaluations

Barnes and Noble

CRAP Design Principles

  • Contrast: The primarily white background with black font does have ample contrast, but so does the dark green font also used throughout.
  • Repetition: The layout of each row of books plus their scroll bar is identical, keeping it consistent.
  • Alignment: The left-hand side has a navigation bar, and the entire right-hand side is book selection, all of which are evenly aligned with appropriate margins.
  • Proximity: Book options are grouped based on different categories that are clearly separated, as is the navigation bar on the left-hand side from the rest of the site.

Childrens International Workshop - Web Fonts Site

This website displays the information on how to include Papyrus, and other fonts from their website in your HTML code.

CRAP Design Principles

  • Contrast: Contrast is high, but the background is a simple white and the font is a simple black.
  • Repetition: Not a strong sense of repition other than the borders in between sections.
  • Alignment: Everything is left-aligned with absolutely no margin.
  • Proximity: Elements are group into sections, but there isn't adequate spacing in between individual elements.
