Come explore North Carolina's Mountains!

North Carolina is part of the Appalachian mountain range, one of the most beautiful mountain ranges in the world. It spans the entirety of western North Carolina and features extremely high altitudes, like Beech Mountian, and more mild elevations like Sugar Mountain.

The towns that span these areas range from quaint, tiny, sparsely populated areas like Lansing, to chic and artsy towns like Asheville. Come find YOUR mountain getaway today!
